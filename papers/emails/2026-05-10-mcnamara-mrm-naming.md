OTIS / SIU / TPS analyses (the per-individual + aggregate-level

provincial comparison, and the Doob-style chi-square family). The

thing to do.

Your contribution, on the other hand, has been real and substantial:
the weekly conversations over the past six months, the foundational
material on distribution theory, and the applied-statistics judgment
you brought from your three decades as the statistician at OPP
headquarters and prior tenure at Statistics Canada. Those are in the
framework's bones. So I'm renaming it:

  MRM = McNamara-Ruhela-Medina

with named contributor roles: M = McNamara (catalyst), R = Ruhela
(principal), M = Medina (reviewer).
